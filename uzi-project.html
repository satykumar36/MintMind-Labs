The Client

Uzi Sports is a premier UK-based retailer that has been supplying top-quality cricket and hockey equipment since 2005. Run by former players who understand the sport inside out, Uzi Sports offers an extensive range of professional gear — from bats and protective equipment to training accessories. With fast dispatch, expert advice, and a dedicated sports-enthusiast community, Uzi Sports has earned an outstanding reputation for both value and service.

The Challenge

Uzi Sports initially came to us for help with website speed and performance. As a brand built on trust, quality, and professional-grade sports gear, they were catering mainly to mobile shoppers, but their customers’ devices struggled to handle Uzi’s expanding product range and detailed equipment listings. When visitors can’t browse or shop smoothly, they don’t convert. And Uzi Sports knew they were losing valuable sales opportunities because of it.

How We Did It

To improve Uzi Sports’ website performance, we began by identifying and removing all unnecessary plugins, scripts, and outdated code that were slowing down mobile page loads. Next, we optimized all product images, brand banners, and other assets for faster rendering and implemented lazy loading to enhance browsing speed on mobile devices.

As a result, Uzi Sports’ bounce rate dropped by nearly 20%, allowing customers to spend more time exploring cricket and hockey gear. This improvement not only created a smoother shopping experience but also helped increase overall conversions and engagement.

To optimize the Uzi Sports shopping experience for mobile, we analyzed data from user behavior and session recordings to redesign Uzi Sports’ category and product detail pages (PDPs). Mobile customers were often overwhelmed by the wide range of cricket and hockey gear, multiple filter options, and detailed product variants, leading to long browsing sessions and fewer purchases.

Using these insights as the foundation for our redesign, we enhanced the user experience (UX) by simplifying navigation and streamlining the filtering process. We reduced the number of filter options visible on mobile category pages and organized product information more clearly. Session data confirmed that these improvements reduced choice overload and helped customers find and buy their desired equipment faster.

Takeaways

Uzi Sports combines a premium sports equipment range with strategic online marketing to create strong demand for its cricket and hockey gear. CherriCode helps Uzi Sports channel that demand through a fast, seamless, and high-converting shopping experience to maximize revenue and keep athletes and sports enthusiasts coming back for more.
