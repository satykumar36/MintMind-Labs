A Hand Tailored Suit – Bespoke Tailoring Platform

A Hand Tailored Suit is a UK-based bespoke tailoring service offering fully custom, made-to-measure garments for men, women, and children. With over 10,000 fabric options, expert style consultations, and limited monthly production to ensure quality and exclusivity, their platform blends online convenience with traditional craftsmanship.

Alexander Nash – Luxury Bespoke Tailoring NYC

Alexander Nash is a premier New York City bespoke tailor delivering luxury custom suits for men and women. Known for precision craftsmanship, handmade basted fittings, and premium fabrics from Scabal, Dormeuil, Ermenegildo Zegna & Loro Piana, their service blends artisanal quality with refined elegance.

Badass Bespokery – Premium Custom Tailoring

Badass Bespokery offers a luxury custom-tailoring service featuring 1,000+ fabrics (Italian, English, Australian) and a full line of tailored items—suits, shirts, shoes, accessories—with a concierge-at-home experience. Clients design every detail from cuffs to linings, with personal stylist support and handcrafted production for a bold, distinctive wardrobe .

Homely – Customizable Sofa & Table Storefront

Homely is a modern, fully customizable eCommerce website for sofas and tables. Designed for a seamless shopping experience, it empowers users to personalize products by size, fabric, color, and features—all within an intuitive, mobile-friendly interface.
